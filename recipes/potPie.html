Pot Pie Recipe

This pot pie recipe is a savory home-style dish that has plenty of bold flavor, and is very simple to make. Just 1 hour of total prep, and cooking will be rewareded with a delicious, filling meal!

  • Butter
  • Yellow or white onion
  • Carrots
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Chicken breast, cubed
  • All purpose flour
  • White wine
  • Low sodium chicken broth
  • Half and half or whole milk
  • Frozen peas
  • Refrigerated pie crust
  • Egg, lightly beaten

Cooking steps


  1. Preheat oven to 425.
  2. Melt the butter in a large skillet. Stir in onion, carrot and celery along with a pinch of salt.
  3. Stir in fresh thyme, followed by chicken. (cook chicken until brown)
  4. Sprinkle with the flour and cook for a further minute
  5. Pour in the broth, and milk or cream
  6. Stir often until sauce is thick
  7. Bake for 35 minutes.
