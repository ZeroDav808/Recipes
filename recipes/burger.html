Pot Pie Recipe

This juicy homemade burger recipe delivers the perfect backyard barbecue experience. With just 30 minutes of prep and cooking time, you'll create restaurant-quality burgers that are perfectly seasoned and cooked to your desired doneness.

  • Ground beef (80/20 lean-to-fat ratio)
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • American or cheddar cheese slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Cooking steps


  1. Gently mix beef with Worcestershire sauce and seasonings, avoid overworking.
  2. Form into 4 equal patties, make a thumb indent in center of each.
  3. Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  4. Cook patties 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness.
  5. Add cheese in last minute of cooking if desired.
  6. Toast buns lightly on grill or in pan.
  7. Assemble burgers with desired toppings and condiments.
