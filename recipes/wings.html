Pot Pie Recipe

These classic buffalo wings bring the perfect balance of heat and tanginess, with a crispy exterior and juicy interior. In just 45 minutes, you'll have restaurant-quality wings ready to serve at your next game day gathering or casual dinner!

  • Chicken wings, split at joints, tips removed
  • Vegetable oil
  • Frank's RedHot sauce
  • Unsalted butter
  • White vinegar
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing for serving
  • Celery sticks
  • Carrots sticks

Cooking steps


  1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Pat wings dry with paper towels.
  2. Toss wings with oil and salt, arrange on baking rack over sheet pan.
  3. Bake for 45-50 minutes until crispy, flipping halfway.
  4. Meanwhile, combine hot sauce, melted butter, vinegar, Worcestershire, cayenne, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  5. When wings are done, transfer to large bowl and toss with sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve immediately with dressing and vegetable sticks.
